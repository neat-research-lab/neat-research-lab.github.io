About the Lab

The NEAT (Next Generation Effective Affective Technology) research group is an inter-disciplinary research group at Western Washington University. Our research focuses on Technology for Neurodivergent Individuals, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and Information and Communication Technologies for Development (ICTD).
